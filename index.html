About viRoomie

viRoomie lets you view video content fully synchronized with friends, family, colleagues or customers. All you need to do is invite them into the viRoom you created. Everyone will see exactly the same thing at the same time.

viRoomie currently supports videos published via these platforms:

  • Netflix
  • Maxdome
  • ARD
  • RBB

Please note that we are in no way associated with any of the aforementioned video platforms. All but YouTube- and BitTubes-Videos are only supported using our Chrome Extension.

 

For businesses we also added a couple of extra options:

Limit who can control the video by enabling the presenter mode. Safe on travel costs and present your content in high definition remotely.

 

It even comes with a laser pointer!
